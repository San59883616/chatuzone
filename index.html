Matokeo ya Darasa la Saba 2023/2024

The National Examinations Council of Tanzania (NECTA) operates under the Government of the United Republic of Tanzania. The council has roles to provide quality education and fair examinations to all Tanzanian students. The National Examination Results (Matokeo ya Darasa La Saba 2023/2024), are just around the corner. Students who sat for Primary School Leaving Examination have a desire to see their results. Since the results will be announced by the National Council will be seen here immediately. We argue students, parents, teachers and guardians to wait patiently until the date to release.
